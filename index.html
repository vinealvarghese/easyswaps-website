Free EV charging is a great perk — until someone parks for 9 hours on a 2-hour charge. EasySwaps EV solves the coordination problem so the benefit is actually fair.

How it works

Simple by design

1

Create or join a group

An admin creates a shared group with an in-app subscription, then invites the team. Members join free — no subscription needed.

2

Queue up

When you arrive at the car park, add yourself to the queue. See exactly where you are and how long the wait is.

3

Start charging

Plug in when a bay opens. Log your start battery percentage and the app estimates when you'll be done.

4

Swap when ready

Get a reminder when your session ends. Move your car and the next person gets their turn automatically.

Features

Everything the queue needs

⚡

Live queue

Real-time position updates so everyone knows exactly where they stand.

🔔

Smart reminders

Automatic notifications when your estimated charge time is up — no more forgetting to swap.

📊

Monthly stats

Transparent usage data and a monthly summary so the queue stays accountable.

👥

Group management

Multiple groups for different sites or teams. Admins manage via subscription; members join free.

🚗

Guest vehicles

Add a car to the queue by reg plate even if the driver isn't in the app.

📣

Ping overdue chargers

Queue members can send a nudge to anyone who has been charging for over 3 hours.
